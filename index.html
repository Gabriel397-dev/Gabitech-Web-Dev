HTML5 Markup

We begin with the foundation of web pages-HTML.
Students learn to build clean, semantic structures using real-life examples (e.g.,portfolios, blog layouts).
We break down elements like headings,lists,forms,links,and media into simple,relatable use cases.
We emphasize best practices,accessibility, and valid markup.

CSS3 Styling

We introduce CSS by virtually demonstrating it's power, turning plain HTML into stunning,responsive designs.
Students master selectors,properties,colors, spacing, fonts, and then move into flexbox,grid,and media Queries.
Every lesson includes live projects to reinforce learning from landing pages to full site themes,

Graphic design

We approach graphic design as both an art and a skill.
Our training is practical,hands-on,and project-based,guiding students from the basics to advanced levels.
